AutoSpotterX Privacy Policy

Home Terms of Service Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

AutoSpotterX ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the AutoSpotterX mobile app (available on Google Play). It applies to all users of the app and covers the data collected through the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account & Profile Data: Username, optional profile photo, account creation date, AutoXP points, and optional bio. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (email/password or Google sign-in). We also store whether you accepted our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy (including acceptance date/version). Your email address is used only for authentication.

User Content: Vehicle photos (low-quality thumbnails stored in Firebase and high-quality photos stored in Cloudinary for feed display), car make/model information (entered manually or via AI detection), AI detection confidence scores, AI identification usage count (tracked in Firebase to enforce free usage limits), notes/captions, timestamps, optional GPS coordinates (stored only on your device - see Section 6.1), country name/code (derived from coordinates), likes (your user ID when you like posts), privacy settings, friend connections and requests (including friend request status, timestamps, and email addresses for pending invitations), notifications (including like notifications with liker information and car details, friend request notifications with requester information, and system notifications), and reports (when you report content).

Device & Usage Data: Technical data from your device, such as device model and operating system version, to ensure compatibility and diagnose issues. We may also collect usage statistics to improve the app.

App Permissions: Camera (to take photos), Storage (to save photos and data), Location (for mapping features), Internet (for cloud sync), and Network State (to check connectivity). You can change or revoke permissions through your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services and Partners

AutoSpotterX uses third-party services (each has its own privacy policy):

  • Firebase (Google): User accounts, database storage, and backend services. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google ML Kit: On-device license plate and face blurring. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini API: Optional AI car identification. Only the photo is sent to Google for identification. Data handling is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Limited free uses per account. We track your usage count in Firebase to enforce usage limits. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Geocoding Service: Converts GPS coordinates to country name/code. Coordinates are sent to Google for lookup (may be collected per Google's Privacy Policy). Only country info is stored in our database. (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudinary: High-quality photo storage and delivery for the public feed. We store high-quality versions of your car photos on Cloudinary (in addition to low-quality thumbnails in Firebase) to provide better image quality in the feed. High-quality photos are only uploaded to Cloudinary if the car appears in the public feed (not hidden by privacy settings or rarity restrictions). High-quality photos may be periodically deleted from Cloudinary for storage management, but low-quality thumbnails remain in Firebase. (Privacy Policy)
  • MapTiler: Map tiles (no access to your location data). (Privacy Policy)
  • MapLibre GL: Map rendering (runs entirely on your device).

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account and profile.
  • Feature Functionality: Process photos (AI detection, blurring, originality detection), manage your car collection, and store spotting data. Photo originality detection analyzes photo metadata (EXIF data) on your device to detect if photos are original or downloaded/screenshots. This analysis happens entirely on your device—no photo data or information is sent to external servers. Photos detected as non-original will not appear in the public feed or Country Spots screen.
  • Public Feed: Display your shared cars in the feed (photo, make/model, likes, country, display name).
  • Country Discovery: Power Country Spots browsing using country codes.
  • Likes and Interactions: Track likes, display counts, and send notifications when someone likes your car.
  • Friend Connections: Manage friends, friend requests, and allow viewing friends' collections.
  • Notifications: Send in-app notifications (likes, friend requests, system updates).
  • Content Moderation: Review and moderate reported content. Delete non-compliant content from our servers (Firestore and Cloudinary) when it violates our Terms of Service. Notify users when their content is removed for policy violations.
  • Gamification: Calculate AutoXP points to unlock features, display XP ranks (Newbie, Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert, Master, Legend), and power leaderboard rankings (top 50 users by AutoXP, showing username, profile photo, and XP).
  • AI Services: Provide car identification via Google Gemini. Usage count is tracked in Firebase (geminiUsage collection) to enforce free usage limits.
  • Privacy Controls: Respect your feed visibility preferences.
  • Mapping: Display car locations on your personal map (coordinates stored locally - see Section 6.1).
  • App Improvement: Analyze anonymous vehicle data to improve car specifications database.

5. Data Security and Storage

We protect your data with:

  • Secure Servers: Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and encrypted storage on Firebase/Google Cloud.
  • Authentication: Secure password authentication (passwords never stored in plain text).
  • Local Processing: Car detection, blurring, and watermarking done on your device. Original images stay on your device.
  • Local Storage: High-quality car photos stored only on your device in encrypted form (AES256-GCM encryption) for your personal viewing. Videos stored only on your device in encrypted form (AES256-GCM encryption). GPS coordinates stored only on your device in app-private storage (protected by Android file system permissions, never uploaded). See Section 6.1 for location details.

6. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. Data sharing is limited to:

  • Service Providers: We engage third-party service providers ("Service Providers") to facilitate our services, provide services on our behalf, perform service-related tasks, or assist us in analyzing how our services are used. These Service Providers include:
    • Firebase (Google): For user authentication, database storage, and backend services. We share username, profile data (profile photo, bio, AutoXP points, account creation date), car collection data, and app usage data. Email is stored in Firebase Authentication only for login purposes and is not shared with our Firestore database or other users.
    • Cloudinary: For high-quality photo storage and delivery in the public feed. We share high-quality versions of uploaded vehicle photos for feed display. High-quality photos are only uploaded to Cloudinary if the car appears in the public feed (not hidden by privacy settings or rarity restrictions). High-quality photos may be periodically deleted from Cloudinary for storage management.
    • Google Services (ML Kit, Gemini API, Geocoding): For on-device processing, optional AI identification, and geocoding services. Data shared is limited to what is necessary for each service (see Section 3 for details).
    • MapTiler: For map tile services. We do not share personal location data with MapTiler, only anonymous map tile requests.
  • Law and Safety: We may disclose your information if required by law (such as a valid subpoena or court order), to comply with legal obligations, or to protect our rights, safety, or property, or that of our users or others.
  • Friends: Friends can see your car collection and profile photo. User IDs are used internally for app functionality (such as managing friendships and navigation) but are not displayed in the UI. They cannot see your location data (see Section 6.1).
  • Public Feed: When you share a car to the public feed (not hidden by privacy settings or rarity restrictions), all users can see: car photo, make/model, like count, country name, timestamp, caption/notes, your display name/profile photo, and AutoXP rank. Exact location is never shown—only country name.
  • Country Spots Screen: Users can browse RARE+ cars by country. Your car photo and make/model are visible. Username/profile info is not shown. Only country name is displayed—no exact location. Only RARE+ cars that were not hidden from the feed appear in country browsing.
  • Like Notifications: When you like someone's car, the person who spotted the car will receive a notification showing that you liked their car (e.g., "Ron liked your Ferrari F40"). The notification includes only your username and car details. Your user ID is used internally for app functionality but is not displayed in the UI.
  • Friend Requests: When you send a friend request to someone (including through the feed and leaderboard), the recipient can see your display name and profile photo in the Friends screen where they can accept or reject the request. They will also receive a notification (e.g., "John sent you a friend request"). Your user ID is used internally for app functionality but is not displayed in the UI. No other personal data is shared.
  • Administrators: Can access user data (photos, profile information, content) for moderation/support. Administrators can delete car photos/spots that do not comply with our Terms of Service. When administrators delete content, it is permanently removed from our servers (Firestore and Cloudinary). Users will be notified via in-app notification when their content is deleted by administrators. Administrators may also hide content from the public feed if content violates policies. Location coordinates are never accessible to administrators (stored only on your device - see Section 6.1).
  • Leaderboard: The app includes a public leaderboard showing the top 50 users by AutoXP. This displays only username, AutoXP points, and profile photo. User IDs are used internally for app functionality (such as sending friend requests) but are not displayed in the UI. All users can view the leaderboard in the Friends screen. Email addresses are never displayed in the leaderboard.

6.1 Map and Location Privacy

Your location privacy is fully protected:

  • Device-Only Storage: GPS coordinates are stored only on your device in SharedPreferences. Never uploaded to our servers. Deleted when you uninstall the app or delete your account.
  • Country Data Only: To enable Country Spots browsing and show which country a car was spotted in, we extract country name/code from coordinates using Google Geocoding (coordinates sent temporarily to Google). Only country name/code is stored on our servers—exact coordinates are not.
  • Completely Private: Only you can see exact locations on your personal map. Friends can see your car photos/details but not locations or map markers.

9. Additional (Optional) Features

AutoSpotterX offers several optional features with extra privacy considerations:

On-Device Detection: The app includes privacy blurring (for license plates and faces) using Google ML Kit on your device. All processing occurs locally, and original images remain on your device. We do not send your photos to external servers for this feature.

Local Video Storage: If you record car videos within the app, they are stored only on your device in encrypted form (using AES-GCM). These videos are never uploaded or shared externally. Deleting the associated car entry or your account will erase these videos.

Cloud AI Identification: As mentioned in Section 3, the optional Google Gemini service can analyze car photos. This is an opt-in feature: you must choose to send a photo to Google for identification. Only the image is sent (no personal data). Data handling is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We track your usage count in Firebase to enforce free usage limits.

Friends & Collection Sharing: If you connect with other users, you can view each other's car collections and profile photos in the app. Friends cannot see your precise car locations or any additional personal data beyond what you've shared (such as display name and avatar). See Section 6.1 for location privacy details.

Public Spot Feed: (See Section 9.3 below.)

9.1 Photo Reporting System

Users can report photos that violate our Terms of Service. When you report content, we collect your user ID, the reported content's ID, and the reason. Our admin team reviews reports and takes appropriate action. When content is reported and found to violate our Terms of Service, it will be deleted from the app and our servers (Firestore and Cloudinary). Users whose content is deleted will be notified via in-app notification. Content may be deleted immediately upon review if it violates our policies. Your identity as the reporter is kept confidential. Report records are kept for moderation purposes only.

9.2 Photo Watermarking

All photos are automatically watermarked with "AutoSpotterX" for branding and content protection. Watermarking is done locally on your device and cannot be disabled.

9.3 Public Spot Feed

The Spot Feed is an optional community feature where users can share and browse car sightings. Feed posts show your car photo (high-quality from Cloudinary), make/model, like count, country name, timestamp, caption/notes (if provided), display name/profile photo, and AutoXP rank. Your user ID is used internally but not displayed. Exact location data is never shown only country name (see Section 6.1).

Upload & Visibility: Low-quality thumbnails are stored in Firebase. High-quality photos upload to Cloudinary only when cars appear in the feed. All users can only share RARE+ cars to the Feed & Country Spots, uncommon cars are automatically hidden. You can prevent uploading cars via Settings → Privacy → "Don't Upload to Feed & Country Spots" (prevents all car uploads) or by toggling "Hide from Feed & Country Spots" when spotting (prevents individual cars). Privacy settings take precedence over rarity restrictions.

Interactions: Other users can like or report your posts. You'll receive notifications when someone likes your car.

9.4 Country Browsing Feature

AutoSpotterX includes a "Country Spots" feature that allows users to browse car sightings organized by country. This feature shows only country-level location data, no precise GPS coordinates or street addresses (see Section 6.1). Users can see car photos and make/model details, but usernames and profile information are not displayed. Only RARE+ cars that were not hidden by the user when uploading appear in country browsing. Country codes are derived from coordinates and stored, but exact coordinates remain on your device.
